About Jennie

  • Profile

    Jennie Kim (born January 16, 1996), known as a singer and rapper. She was born and raised in South Korea, Jennie studied in New Zealand for five years before returning to South Korea in 2010. Jennie auditioned for YG Entertainment that same year and succeeding in joining the label as a trainee. On June 1, 2016, Jennie was the first member to be revealed for YG Entertainment's newest girl group.

  • Solo Career

    In November 2018, Jennie made her debut as a solo artist with the single “Solo”. The song was commercially successful, topping both the Gaon Digital Chart and Billboard World Digital Songs chart.

  • Fashion

    Jennie was chosen to become the ambassador of Chanel Korea in June 2018.
    In 2019, Jennie became the face of Hera, a South Korean luxury beauty brand.
    On April 2020, it was announced that Jennie had collaborated with Gentle Monster, a South Korean luxury eyewear brand to create a collection called Jentle Home.
    On May 2021, Jennie became the face of Calvin Klein’s Spring 2021 collection.
