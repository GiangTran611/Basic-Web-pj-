About Rosé

  • Profile

    Roseanne Park (born 11 February 1997), known as Rosé, is a singer and dancer. She was born in New Zealand and raised in Australia, Rosé signed with South Korean label YG Entertainment following a successful audition in 2012 and trained for four years before debuting as a member of the girl group Black Pink in August 2016.

  • Solo Career

    In March 2021, Rosé made her solo debut with her single album R. The album sold 448,089 copies in its first week, the highest for a Korean female soloist. Lead single “On the Ground” was also a commercial success, topping the Billboard Global 200 and peaking in the top five domestically.

  • Fashion

    In July 2020, Rosé was named the global ambassador for Yves Saint Laurent- a French luxury fashion brand, its first global ambassador in 59 years. She was the global face of Saint Laurent's Fall 2020 campaign. In January 2021, Rosé became the muse for luxury cosmetics brand Yves Saint Laurent Beauté.
    In April 2021, Rosé was revealed as Tiffany & Co’s (a French-owned American luxury jewelry and specialty retailer) newest global ambassador. or.
