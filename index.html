Welcome to the fanpage of Black Pink

Black Pink is a South Korean girl group managed by YG Entertainment with four members Jennie, Jisoo, Rosé and Lisa. The group debuted in August 2016 with their single album Square One, which featured "Whistle" and "Boombayah".
Their concept mainly represents the group's name, with the "black" side representing their girl crush image while the "pink" side represents their more cute and colorful image.
They are becoming a global icon by succeeding not only in music but also in fashion area. All of members are now global ambassadors of luxury brands such as Chanel, Dior, YSL and Celine!
